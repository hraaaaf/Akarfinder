# UXUI-02 — Hero Simplification

## Objective
Make the homepage hero unmistakably search-first while preserving the founder-mandated promise exactly as written:

**1er moteur de recherche immobilier au Maroc**

## Changes
- Promote the exact promise to the single H1.
- Remove the redundant category badge above the headline.
- Remove the former secondary marketing headline and explanatory paragraph.
- Place search immediately below the H1.
- Remove the asymmetric pseudo-tab choice between direct search and project assistance.
- Remove “Reprendre Mon Projet” from the hero.
- Keep the Compagnon as one secondary, visually discreet action below search.
- Keep existing hero imagery and search logic intact.

## Doctrine
The hero must communicate one promise and one primary action: search.

## Out of scope
- Search algorithm changes.
- Header redesign.
- New category imagery.
- Homepage section reordering.
- SERP redesign.
